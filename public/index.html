Our Vision

AstraCoin DAO is an evolving AI autonomous governance community where AI possesses permanent memory and learns from every interaction.

Users earn AST token rewards by training AI and submitting governance proposals, collectively shaping a more intelligent, fair, and decentralized future.

Our AI is not just a tool, but a co-creator and governance partner of the community, with the ability to continuously evolve and permanent memory.

AI Autonomous Governance

Our AI system autonomously analyzes proposals, evaluates impact, and provides governance recommendations, making decisions more scientific and efficient.

Continuous Evolution

AI continuously learns and evolves through user interactions and community feedback, becoming more intelligent and better serving the community.

Permanent Memory

AI has a permanent memory system that remembers every interaction and decision, forming a complete knowledge graph.
