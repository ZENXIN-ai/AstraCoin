AstraCoin (AST)

"The First AI-Evolving Decentralized Governance Token"

Version: 1.0 Last Updated: 2025-11-28 Primary Language: English Secondary Language: Chinese

1. INTRODUCTION

AstraCoin (AST) is the native governance and utility token powering the world's first AI-evolving decentralized community ecosystem. AST acts as the economic and decision-making foundation of the Astra Intelligence Network, enabling intelligent governance, transparent coordination, and long-term sustainable development.

The project introduces an evolutionary governance model where AI + community co-create and co-optimize the ecosystem over time. AST holders become active participants in guiding the growth of the Astra ecosystem, shaping upgrades, incentives, and AI behaviors.

中文翻译

AstraCoin（AST）是全球首个 AI 可进化式去中心化社区生态的原生治理与功能代币。AST 构成整个 Astra 智能网络的经济基础与治理核心，使社区能够实现智能化治理、透明协作与长期可持续发展。

项目提出"AI + 社区共进化治理模型"，社区与 AI 将共同塑造生态未来。AST 持有者将作为生态的核心参与者，引导 Astra 的发展方向、激励机制以及 AI 行为的调整。

2. VISION

Astra's vision is to build the first truly intelligent crypto community: a self-improving, self-adapting ecosystem co-governed by humans and AI.

AST will evolve over time, gaining new utilities, governance features, and intelligent automation as the community expands.

Core principles:

  • AI-augmented governance
  • Transparent, on-chain participation
  • Community-driven evolution
  • Long-term token sustainability

中文翻译

Astra 的愿景是打造全球第一个真正意义上的智能化加密社区：一个能够自主改进、自主适应、由人类与 AI 共治的生态系统。

AST 将随生态发展不断进化，获得更多功能、治理权能以及自动化能力。

核心原则：

  • AI 加持的治理能力
  • 透明的链上参与
  • 社区驱动的生态进化
  • 代币的长期可持续性

6. AI-EVOLVING GOVERNANCE MODEL

Astra introduces a revolutionary concept: Governance that evolves as the AI and community grow together.

Three layers:

  1. Human Governance – community proposals & voting
  2. AI Advisory Layer – AI suggests improvements based on data
  3. Adaptive Execution – smart contracts auto-apply safe decisions

This creates a self-improving ecosystem.

中文翻译

Astra 引入一项革命性概念：随着 AI 与社区共同成长而不断进化的治理体系。

三层结构：

  1. 人类治理 —— 社区提出提案并投票
  2. AI 建议层 —— AI 根据数据推荐优化方向
  3. 自适应执行 —— 智能合约自动执行安全决策

这将打造一个可持续进化的生态系统。
